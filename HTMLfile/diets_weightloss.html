Weight loss


Meet your dietitian!


In order to lose weight, you need to meet a good dietitian.

That's why Sport and diet provides you two dietitians to help you.

They will teach you how to eat better and healthier.

Here is a little presentation of our two professionnals. If you want to meet them you simply have to register yourself. First meeting is free, so don't hesitate anymore !


Julie HAVERD

Dietitian


"Hi, I'm Julie. Are you trying to lose weight without succeeding? Have you ever tried ten diets without losing a single gram? I am the dietitian you need! My method? I get to know you, I study your eating habits and create a program especially for you. My diets are not based on deprivation, but on a food balance. What does that mean? Simply that I will teach you how to eat healthier, without limitng the amount on your plate so that you don't feel like starving!"


Alex DEBERG

Dietitian for athlets


"Are you an athlete and you need a special diet? I am the dietitian you need. I study your sports program to offer you a meal schedule adapted to your daily life and your nutritional needs. My asset? I only offer diets without dietary supplements. I want you to eat healthy. Your health is my priority!"



Other diets


If you think that you don't need a dietitian, Sport and Diet also have three different programs for you that you can test alone :


Program 10 D

Ten days to lose maximum weight


Ideal when the summer holidays arrive without warning. This program is nevertheless very hard and very strict. But it is still affordable for everyone. Indeed, it can also be the first step of a long-lasting diet. This is a restrictive diet for the first five days followed by a rebalancing of the diet. We will let you discover it for a price of only $9.95.


Beginner

Learn how to practice a sport regularly


This program must be preceded by 10 D because it requires a good food balance. In this pack, you will discover many sports that help you lose weight, you will benefit from a sports calendar to help you in your weight loss as well as a guide for your meals. You will also find a notebook that will allow you to follow your progress, the number of calories absorbed and eliminated. You can try it for $49.99.


Advanced

For intermediate and high levels


This program must also be done after 10 D. It is for active people who are looking for more muscle. This is a stricter diet that must be followed by meal guides and sports exercise planning. It is for only one month. Don't forget to drink and take showers ! But first and foremore : good luck! It’s priced at $25.99.
